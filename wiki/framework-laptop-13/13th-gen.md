# Framework Laptop 13 (13th Gen Intel Core)

## Specs
### CPU
Framework Laptop 13 (13th Gen Intel Core) is available with three CPU options) [^1]
#### Intel Core i5-1340P
##### Cores 
12 cores (4x Raptor Cove "P Cores" + 8x Gracemont "E Cores") [^2] [^3]

16 threads [^7] 

##### Cache 
Cache L3:	12 MB (shared) [^4]

##### Clockspeed 
P cores: 1.90 GHz base frequency, turbo boost up to 4.60 GHz 

E cores: 1.90 GHz base frequenct, turbo boost up to 3.40 GHz [^5]

##### Integrated Graphics 
Iris Xe Graphics 80EU [^6] when paired with dual channel memory. Otherwise, Intel UHD Graphics. [^14]

#### Intel Core i7-1360P
##### Cores 
12 cores (4x Raptor Cove "P Cores" + 8x Gracemont "E Cores")[^16] [^17]

16 threads [^18] 

##### Cache 
Cache L3:	18 MB (shared) [^19]

##### Clockspeed 
P cores: 2.20 GHz base frequency, turbo boost up to 5.00 GHz 

E cores: 1.60 GHz base frequenct, turbo boost up to 3.70 GHz [^20]

##### Integrated Graphics 
Iris Xe Graphics 96EU [^21] when paired with dual channel memory. Otherwise, Intel UHD Graphics. [^22]


#### Intel Core i7-1370P
##### Cores 
14 cores (6x Raptor Cove "P Cores" + 8x Gracemont "E Cores") [^24] [^25]

20 threads [^26]

##### Cache 
Cache L3:	24 MB (shared) [^27]

##### Clockspeed 
P cores: 1.90 GHz base frequency, turbo boost up to 5.20 GHz 

E cores: 1.40 GHz base frequenct, turbo boost up to 3.90 GHz [^28]

##### Integrated Graphics 
Iris Xe Graphics 96EU [^29] when paired with dual channel memory. Otherwise, Intel UHD Graphics. [^30]

#### For All Processor Options 
##### Cache
Cache L1:	80 KB (per core) 

Cache L2:	2 MB (per core) 

E-Core L1:	96 KB (per core) 

E-Core L2:	4 MB (per module) [^8]

100Â°C [^12]

#### Memory Support 
Max: 2x 32 GB SODIMM DDR4-3200 JEDEC (single DIMM, smaller capacities, and slower speeds should work) [^9]

#### Connectivity 
Two Intel Thunderbolt 4 controllers integrated into the CPU [^10]

PCIe 4.0 x4 m.2 key M slot. [^13] 

Intel Wi-Fi 6E AX210, Intel Wi-Fi 6E AX210 with vProBluetooth 5.3 [^11]
